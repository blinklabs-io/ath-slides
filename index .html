Boosting Cardano Ecosystem Exposure through Events

Harrison Nguyen

The Internet may have an advantage over real life in terms of scalability. However, human connection is truer in face-to-face interaction than in any other form. A strong and sustainable ecosystem depends a lot on the relationship between the builders not just in work, online but also in real life. Join us and let's discuss how we can improve human connection for Cardano's in-person and hybrid events.
